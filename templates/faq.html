Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Sugra Games is an indie game marketplace that connects developers with players. Our name comes from the Irish word "s√∫gradh", relating to play. We provide a platform where indie developers can showcase and sell their games directly to players.

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar. You'll need to choose between a Gamer account (for purchasing games) or a Developer account (for publishing games).

For Developers

After creating a Developer account, you can submit your game through the Developer Dashboard. Your game will go through a review process before being published. Make sure to include high-quality screenshots, a detailed description, and set an appropriate price.

After submission, your game will be reviewed by our team. You'll receive a message in your Developer Inbox when the review is complete, either approving your game for publication or providing feedback if changes are needed.

For Gamers

After purchase, games are available in your Library. You can access your Library through the Gamer Dashboard or directly from the navigation menu when logged in. Each game will have a download button to get the game files.
